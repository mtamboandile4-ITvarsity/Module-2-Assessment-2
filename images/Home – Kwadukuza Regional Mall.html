About

KwaDukuza Mall is located on the lush green North Coast of KwaZulu Natal in KwaDukuza (also known as Stanger) is positioned just 500m from the R74 off-ramp, over the N2.

The 29, 000m² development which has completely transformed the existing town is owned by well-known entrepreneur, Vivian Reddy and opened on 28th September 2018. The mall which is home to 69 stores is anchored by popular national retailers such as Edgars, Woolworths, Checkers and Pick n Pay, whilst further complemented by an array of fashion, food and homeware offerings which has brought shopping convenience to the town.

In line with contemporary town-planning, the mall is a key component of a bigger mixed-use development, and boosts 971 free, open air parking bays, a Muslim prayer room, modern ablutions facilities with separate paraplegic and family rooms.

management

broil property group

KwaDukuza Mall is proudly managed by Africa’s leading property services provider, Broll Property Group.

Founded in 1975, Broll Property Group is one of Africa’s leading commercial property services company with offices in major cities and towns in South Africa as well as operations in Ghana, Indian Ocean Islands, Kenya, Malawi, Mozambique, Namibia and Nigeria and provides real estate services in other African countries.

The Broll Property Group are affiliates to the CBRE, a renowned global commercial real estate and investment firm which enables us to offer unrivalled local expertise and global market knowledge with the sole purpose of maximising the potential of your property.

Broll offers services which include asset management and consulting, corporate real estate services, facilities management, industrial, investment and office broking, property and project management, retail leasing and projects, research, shopping centre management, valuation and advisory services, own patented Broll-Online property-management software solution and a property search function with a vast database of properties across South Africa and Sub-Saharan Africa where they have operations.

Ownership

KwaDukuza Mall is owned by chairman and founder of the Edison Power Group, Vivian Reddy, who started his business career from humble beginnings 36 years ago in the electrical contracting industry.
Through difficulties and many testing times, he finds himself years later, the head of a multi-faceted multinational conglomerate that has not only pioneered the way for various industrial and technological advancements and vast diversification, but also contributed to great to socio-economic upliftment.

Through the closely related nature of the electrical and construction industries, Vivian saw the opportunity that presented itself in the form of property development and his interests now entail the acquisition and development of strategically placed property at rapidly developing nodes throughout South Africa.

parking

KwaDukuza Mall offers ample free (open air) parking bays for shoppers’ convenience. We also have designated bays available for persons with disabilities which may also be used by the aged and at the discretion of Centre Management by Moms with Tots.

Parking on the premises is done so entirely at the parkers own risk and we urge all shoppers to be aware of remote jamming.

Wheel clamping is also in operation and a release fee of R500 is payable by any parker found parking illegally on-site.
